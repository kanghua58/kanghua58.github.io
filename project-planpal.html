A Smart AI Agent that communicates information

With a focus on personalized guidance and motivation, PlanPal introduces a team of 4 AI coaches - Master Loong, Frankie, Paw and Whisper - each with unique interaction styles and motivational techniques, to cater to the diverse needs and preferences of users. 1. Motivate, Learn, and Achieve with Personalized Goal Gurus

PlanPal is an AI-powered learning tool designed to revolutionize the way individuals set and achieve their personal goals. With a focus on personalized guidance and motivation, PlanPal introduces a team of 4 AI coaches (Master Loong the dragon, Frankie the lion, Paw the puppy and Whisper the spirit), each with unique interaction styles and motivational techniques, to cater to the diverse needs and preferences of its users. By leveraging advanced AI technologies, PlanPal offers tailored feedback, progress tracking, and adaptive learning experiences to keep users engaged and committed to their goals (e.g. getting in shape).

Motivation Style

PlanPal helps users identify their unique motivation style through a personalized assessment, enabling the system to tailor guidance and support to be personalized.

Self-assessment

Users complete a self-assessment to evaluate their current fitness level, allowing PlanPal to create a customized starting point for their fitness journey.

SMART Goal Setting

PlanPals guide users through setting Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound (SMART) goals, ensuring goals are well-defined and realistic.

Goal Breakdown

The "Progress Track Galaxy" feature visually breaks down users' SMART goals into smaller, manageable milestones, providing a clear roadmap for success.

Goal Tracking

Users can easily monitor their daily progress and tasks within the "Progress Track Galaxy," helping them stay on track and accountable.

Progress Log

Users can log their progress and engage in motivational conversations with their AI coaches through a dedicated group chat feature.

Community

The community allows users to view the progress of other friends and participate in peer evaluations, fostering a sense of collaboration and support.

Daily Challenge

PlanPal presents users with daily challenge questions to reinforce their learning, encourage reflection, and promote a growth mindset.

Weekly Email

PlanPal sends users a weekly email summarizing their progress and highlighting key learning outcomes, providing a regular source of motivation and reflection.

Notification/Widgets

PlanPal utilizes notifications and widgets to send timely reminders, updates, and encouragement, ensuring that users remain engaged and committed to their goals.

Exploration

Learners can quickly access supplementary information that is helpful but not mandatory, allowing them to deepen their understanding of their goal at their own pace.

Quick Reference

A quick reference to the SMART framework is always available on the screen, providing a convenient reminder of the key components of SMART goals.

2. The New Year Resolution that We Never Follow through

The Problem: People find it hard to move towards long-term goals

Many individuals struggle to follow through on their New Year's resolutions, particularly those related to fitness and health. This common challenge can be attributed to several learning gaps, including a lack of proper goal-setting skills, difficulty in maintaining motivation, and limited knowledge of effective strategies for achieving long-term objectives. These learning gaps affect a wide range of stakeholders, from young adults to working professionals and retirees, who find themselves falling short of their fitness goals despite their initial enthusiasm and commitment.

Our Audience and Context: People with Fitness Goals

We find that America has a culture of working out. It's common to see people around set fitness goals. So we decided on setting fitness goals as our touch points.

Through structured interview with 4 learner with varying levels of fitness knowledge and experience. The Overwhelmed Student, for example, has a lot of fitness knowledge but struggles with execution due to time constraints and other priorities. On the other hand, the Adaptive Fitness Enthusiast is more flexible in their approach to fitness and doesn't adhere to a strict schedule, while the Determined Busy Health Seeker tends to get discouraged if they don't see quick results. Lastly, the Spontaneous Fitness Beginner is new to fitness and acknowledges that staying consistent will be a challenge. These insights highlight the importance of creating a personalized learning experience that caters to the unique needs and challenges of each user persona, providing them with the tools and support they need to set and achieve their fitness goals effectively.

Our learner persona, Ken, is a representative of our target audience. Ken is a busy professional who sets high standards for himself but often feels disappointed when he fails to meet his fitness goals. This negatively impacts his motivation to continue, and his busy schedule and tendency to procrastinate make it difficult for him to stay accountable and make consistent progress. We chose to focus on the domain of fitness because it is a common area of interest for many individuals, and the challenges faced by Ken are representative of those experienced by a significant portion of our target audience.

The Significance of the Topic: Why Fitness?

Learning how to set and achieve fitness goals is crucial for improving physical health and well-being. Poor physical health and well-being can lead to a range of negative outcomes, including increased risk of chronic diseases, reduced quality of life, and increased healthcare costs. By helping individuals like Ken develop effective goal-setting skills, we can empower them to take control of their health and well-being, leading to better long-term outcomes and a higher quality of life.

Effective goal-setting skills provide individuals with direction, motivation, and a sense of purpose in their fitness journey. The absence of well-defined goals can make it difficult to measure success and adjust strategies when necessary, potentially leading to frustration and disengagement from fitness activities altogether.

Our Goal: Help Learner Set and Achieve Fitness Goals

The primary goal of our project is to develop an AI-powered learning tool, PlanPal, that addresses the learning gaps identified above. We aimed to create a mobile app that provides personalized guidance and support to help users set and achieve their fitness goals. By leveraging AI technology and evidence-based learning strategies, we sought to create an engaging and effective learning experience that empowers individuals to develop the skills and knowledge necessary for long-term success in their fitness journey. Ultimately, our goal was to make a meaningful impact on the lives of our users by helping them overcome the common challenges associated with New Year's resolutions and achieve their health and wellness objectives.

5. Reflection: Learning in the Age of AI

More Effective User Testing

Moving forward, the logical next steps in studying PlanPal's effectiveness would be to implement pre- and post-tests with short answer questions and grading rubrics to measure learning gains. This process will provide a clearer understanding of the app's impact on users' knowledge and skills related to goal-setting and achievement.

Scaling to Goal Setting in Any Domain

The design approach used in PlanPal has broad implications and applications beyond the domain of fitness. The principles and strategies employed could be adapted to support goal-setting and achievement in various areas, such as education, personal development, career growth, and relationships. Looking into the near future, by incorporating data-driven algorithms and more advanced large language models, PlanPal as a learning tool could have the potential to enhance goal accomplishment universally, leading to improved outcomes at a larger scale.

Takeaways

Leveraging AI to Simulate Social Presence

PlanPal harnesses the power of AI to create a sense of social presence, which has been shown to enhance goal performance. By making goals public to AI coaches that simulate real-life accountability partners, users feel more committed and motivated to achieve their objectives. This approach taps into the human need for social support and encouragement.

Best Practices and Pitfalls of AI in Educational Contexts

As AI continues to revolutionize the educational landscape, it is crucial to understand the best practices and potential pitfalls of incorporating this technology into learning experiences. PlanPal serves as a valuable case study. It would be intriguing to see the app being developed and run experiments with.
